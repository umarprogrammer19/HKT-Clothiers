At HKT Clothiers, Every Stitch Tells a Story

 

At HKT, helping people look their best is part of our heritage. Inspired by my father’s textile business in Malta and my uncle Cliff Vaswani’s founding of HKT in 1970, we’re built on hard work, exceptional service, and fine craftsmanship.

Our master tailors and style consultants create personalized garments that reflect each client’s individuality. By understanding your style, needs, and preferences, we ensure a perfect fit that leaves you looking sharp and feeling confident,every time.
